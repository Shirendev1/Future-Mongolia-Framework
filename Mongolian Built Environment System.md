# Mongolia Built Environment System

## Vision
Develop a human-centered and climate-adaptive construction standard for Mongolia.

## Key Principles

1. Human Scale Cities
Cities should prioritize walkability and community spaces rather than high-rise density.

2. Climate Adaptive Architecture
Buildings must respond to Mongolia's extreme climate conditions.

3. Circular Construction
Promote reuse of materials and low-carbon construction methods.

4. Regional Design
Architecture should reflect Mongolia’s different ecological regions.

5. Smart Infrastructure
Integrate energy monitoring, water recycling, and digital infrastructure.
