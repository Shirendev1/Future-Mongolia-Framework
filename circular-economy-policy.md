# Circular Economy Policy for Mongolia

## Overview

The Circular Economy Policy aims to reduce waste, increase resource efficiency, and support sustainable economic development in Mongolia.

Instead of a linear system of extraction, production, and disposal, a circular economy promotes reuse, recycling, and regeneration of resources.

## Key Objectives

- Reduce dependence on raw resource extraction
- Promote recycling industries
- Encourage sustainable production
- Support green technology and innovation
- Improve waste management systems

## Policy Directions

### 1. Recycling Infrastructure
Develop national recycling facilities for metals, plastics, and electronic waste.

### 2. Mining Waste Reuse
Encourage the reuse of mining waste in construction and manufacturing.

### 3. Green Industry Development
Support industries that use recycled materials and sustainable processes.

### 4. Waste Management Systems
Implement nationwide waste sorting and collection systems.

### 5. Circular Innovation
Encourage research and innovation in sustainable materials and technologies.

## Expected Outcomes

- Reduced environmental impact
- Diversified economy
- New green industries
- Sustainable resource use
