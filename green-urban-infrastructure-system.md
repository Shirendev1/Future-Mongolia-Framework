## European Green Infrastructure Lessons

Mongolia can learn from European urban sustainability systems, particularly from countries such as the Czech Republic.

### Key Areas

1. Wastewater Treatment Systems
Modern biological treatment plants ensure water purification and environmental protection.

2. Urban Green Infrastructure
Cities integrate parks, green corridors, and urban forests.

3. Sustainable Waste Management
Waste separation and recycling systems reduce environmental impact.

4. Public Transport Systems
Efficient tram, metro, and electric bus systems reduce car dependency.
