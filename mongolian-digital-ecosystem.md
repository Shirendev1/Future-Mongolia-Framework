# Mongolian Digital Ecosystem

## Overview
This concept explores how Mongolia could develop a stronger national digital ecosystem based on the .mn domain and Mongolian language support.

## Key Ideas
- National email services (example: name@mongol.mn)
- Strengthening the .mn domain ecosystem
- Mongolian language support online
- Open digital infrastructure for Mongolian users

## Why It Matters
Many countries have strong national digital ecosystems. Mongolia currently relies heavily on global platforms. Developing local digital infrastructure can strengthen digital sovereignty and cultural presence online.

## Connection to Global Internet Governance
This concept aligns with discussions around:
- Universal Acceptance
- Internationalized Domain Names
- Internet Governance communities such as ICANN
